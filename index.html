You play as the Hunter, but before that you were a normal person, who came to Yharnam to seek the healing blood

This blood was like no other, tainted. Turning men and women to beasts. You as the Hunter is to kill the beasts.
Being enlisted as a Hunter means you may not die, each time you die, you return to the hunters dream, trapped.
